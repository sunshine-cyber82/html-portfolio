The Best Animes According to Sunshine

My top 3 best animes i have watched.

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer is a great anime thanks to its stunning animation, emotional story, and strong character development. The action scenes are visually amazing, and the sibling bond between Tanjiro and Nezuko adds real heart.

Fire Force

Fire Force is a solid anime with intense action, unique fire-based powers, and stylish animation. Its world-building is creative, and the fast-paced battles keep things exciting. The mix of humor, mystery, and cool visuals makes it stand out.

Dragon Ball

Dragon Ball is a classic anime known for its iconic characters, epic battles, and massive influence on the genre. Its mix of action, humor, and growth makes it a timeless favorite for many fans.
